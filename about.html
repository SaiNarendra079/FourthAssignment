About Us

Welcome to Zeeshan, where culinary tradition meets modern flavors.

Our journey began in 2007 with a passion for great food and a desire to create a warm and welcoming dining space.

Our mission is to provide exceptional food experiences with quality ingredients and a touch of creativity.

At Zeeshan, we serve authentic Hyderabadi cuisine prepared by Hyderabadi chefs at our 19 Pan India branches located in Pune (3 Branch), Visakhapatnam (4 Branch), Kakinada, Shrikakulam, Rajahmundry, Chennai (2 Branch), Tandur (2 Branch), Vikarabad, Mahabubnagar (2 Branch), Narayanpet and Hyderabad. We take great care in preparing each dish, using only the finest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to deliver an authentic dining experience that is second to none. Our friendly staff and cozy ambiance at all our Branches are sure to make you feel Special. Our menu features Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, Kebabs, Curries, Sea Food, Hyderabadi Sweet Dish, and Arabian Cuisine Mandi. Come to Zeeshan and savor the true flavors of Hyderabad today.
